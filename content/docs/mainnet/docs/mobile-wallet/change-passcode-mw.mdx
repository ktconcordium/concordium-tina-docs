If you want to change your passcode or enable/disable your biometrics (on a mobile device), following the directions below for your wallet.

<Callout
  body={<>
You can't change your passcode, password, or biometrics if you have pending identities or accounts in your wallet.
  </>}
  variant="info"
/>


<accordion
  heading="Concordium Wallet"

2. Tap **Update Passcode & Biometrics**.

3. On the next screen tap **Continue**.

![screen with settings menu](/../images/cryptoX/cryptox-update-passcode-and-biometrics.png)
4. Enter your old passcode or biometrics.

<accordion
  heading="Desktop Wallet"
  docText={<>
1. Click ![gear wheel](/../images/desktop-wallet/dw-settings-button.png) to go to the Settings page.

2. Click **Change wallet password**.

3. Enter your current password. **You cannot change the password unless you know the current password.** Then enter your new password and confirm your new password. Click Change password.

![password change dialog](/../images/desktop-wallet/dw-change-pw.png)
  </>}
