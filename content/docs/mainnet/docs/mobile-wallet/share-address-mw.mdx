(share-address-mw)=


If you need to share your account address with someone, you can find the address on the account page.

<accordion
  heading="Concordium Wallet"
  docText={<>
1. Tap **Receive** on the main screen.

2. You see a QR code and your address. To share your address:

   - You can let someone scan your QR code if they have Concordium Wallet installed.

   - You can tap **Share** and then share the address using one of the share options.

   - You can tap **Copy** to copy the address and then paste it somewhere of your own choosing.


![screen with QR code and account address shown](/images/cryptoX/cryptox-QR-code-new.png)
  </>}

/>

<accordion
  heading="Concordium Wallet for Web"

1. Select your account in the dropdown menu and click **Receive**.

2. Share your address:

   - You can let someone scan your QR code if they have the Concordium Wallet installed.

   - You can click **COPY** to copy the address and then paste it somewhere of your own choosing.
