title: "Show wallet private key"
---

(show-wallet-private-key)=


Concordium Wallet offers you to [restore your wallet](/docs/mainnet/docs/guides/recovery#backup-import-recover) using a wallet private key. After doing that, you will have access to viewing the wallet private key via the *Wallet Settings* (tap the gear icon in the lower right of the main screen to open Wallet Settings):

1. On the Wallet Settings screen, Tap **Wallet private key**.

2. Tap **Show wallet private key**.

   ![screen with seed phrase](/../images/cryptoX/show-wallet-private-key.png)

3. Enter your passcode when prompted.

4. Now you can tap **Copy to clipboard** to copy the key to the clipboard.
