(show-seed-phrase)=


Your seed phrase is the access key to all the funds in your wallet. It is important that you store a copy of it in a safe location.

<accordion
  heading="Concordium Wallet"
  docText={<>
1. Open *Wallet Settings* by tapping the gear icon in the lower right of the main screen.

2. Tap **Seed Phrase**.

3. Tap **Show seed phrase**.

![screen with seed phrase](/images/cryptoX/cryptox-show-seed-phrase.png)
4. Enter your passcode when prompted.

5. Now you can tap **Copy to clipboard** to copy the seed phrase to the clipboard.
  </>}
  image=""
  fullWidth={true}
/>

<accordion
  heading="Concordium Wallet for Web"
  docText={<>
1. Open the menu and select **Seed phrase**.

2. Enter your passcode and click **Show seed phrase**.

![](/images/browser-wallet/new/show_seedphrase.png)
3. Now you can click **Copy seed phrase** to copy the seed phrase to the clipboard.
